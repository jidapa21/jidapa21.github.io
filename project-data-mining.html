Movie Genre Association Analysis

Discovering frequent genre co-occurrence patterns using FP-Growth and Apriori algorithms.

Overview

Project Overview

Analyzed relationships between movie genres using Association Rule Mining to identify frequently co-occurring genres and behavioral viewing patterns. Applied frequent itemset mining and association rule generation to discover genre combinations commonly appearing together in animation-based movie datasets.

Objective

Objectives

  • Identify frequent genre combinations in animation-based movies
  • Apply FP-Growth and Apriori association rule mining techniques
  • Analyze Support, Confidence, and Lift metrics for discovered rules
  • Interpret patterns for potential content recommendation systems
Process

Mining Process

  • Step 1 — Data retrieval and cleaning of the movie dataset
  • Step 2 — Attribute selection focusing on the genre column
  • Step 3 — Nominal to text conversion for genre values
  • Step 4 — Tokenization of multi-genre strings into individual items
  • Step 5 — Frequent itemset generation via FP-Growth algorithm
  • Step 6 — Association rule creation and metric evaluation
